What is Chi Ladies Hack?

Chicago Women Developers, Girl Develop It Chicago, PyLadies Chicago, RailsBridge Chicago, Women Who Code Chicago and Write/Speak/Code Chicago invite you to participate in a hackathon for anyone who identifies as a woman or as non-binary.

Chi Ladies Hackathon will take place this April 1st and 2nd. Women developers from all different skill levels and backgrounds will get together to learn and hack on projects for local non-profits and minority-owned businesses.
